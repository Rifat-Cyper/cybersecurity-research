# Healthcare Cybersecurity Challenges: A Review

## Abstract
The healthcare sector has become a primary target for cyberattacks due to the increasing value of sensitive patient data and the rapid adoption of digital health technologies. This review examines key cybersecurity challenges, recent attack trends, and the impact of emerging threats on healthcare systems.

## Introduction
Healthcare organizations are undergoing rapid digital transformation, including the adoption of electronic health records (EHRs), telemedicine, and cloud-based systems. While these advancements improve efficiency and accessibility, they also introduce significant cybersecurity risks.

## Key Cybersecurity Threats
- Ransomware attacks targeting hospitals and healthcare providers  
- Phishing campaigns exploiting human vulnerabilities  
- Data breaches involving patient records  
- Attacks on critical infrastructure and medical devices  

## Challenges in Healthcare Security
- Limited cybersecurity awareness among staff  
- Legacy systems and outdated infrastructure  
- Increased attack surface during COVID-19  
- Lack of proactive security strategies  

## Conclusion
Healthcare cybersecurity requires a shift from reactive to proactive strategies to ensure resilience against evolving cyber threats.
